Team Wingman

Executive Summary

Our application is called "Wingman". Wingman has your back.


It is an augmented intelligence application for the flight crews - pilot, air traffic control, ground crew and maintenance.


This application is personalized for each crew member to receive the information they need at the exact time that they need it. It will be an integrated tool across multiple computer systems and mobile devices.


With connection to the internet, sensors, and voice recognition commands, crew members will have an experience of being on a state-of-the-art Star Trek spacecraft.
