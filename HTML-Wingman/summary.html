Team Wingman

Summary

By touch, or voice commands, Wingman gives real-time access to key personnel, crew member location updates, airport information, weather systems, flight schedules, aircraft information, communications systems, baggage loading, etc.


This application will orchestrate a seamless, trouble-free flight giving crew members the ability to prepare for each flight, get enough rest, do their job with confidence and exceed customer expectation. An excellent customer experience allows airlines to build customer loyalty, drive down cost for marketing, and charge a premium for offering a superior service.


The customer experience is the brand of the company. By sending automatic, real-time messages inflight, the maintenance crew was ready and repairing the faulty light while the plane was being refueled, baggage loaded and passengers on-boarding. Delay for the next leg was minimized providing for happy customers.
